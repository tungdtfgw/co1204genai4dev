Diverse Generative Architectures

  • Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs):
    • Operating Principle: Consists of two competing neural networks: Generator (creates fake data) and Discriminator (distinguishes real from fake data). This process helps the Generator create increasingly realistic data.
    • Applications: Image, video, music generation, and other data types.
  • Variational Autoencoders (VAEs):
    • Operating Principle: Learn a compressed representation (latent representation) of data and then use this representation to generate new data.
    • Applications: Image generation, data dimensionality reduction, and other generative tasks.
