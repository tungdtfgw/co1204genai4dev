Prompt Engineering

  • Concept and Importance: Prompt Engineering is the process of designing and optimizing prompts to control the output of Gen AI models. The quality of the prompt directly affects the quality of results. A good prompt can make the difference between a useless result and an excellent one.
  • Basic Prompt Engineering Techniques:
    • Zero-shot prompting: Direct requests without examples.
    • Few-shot prompting: Providing a few examples to guide the model.
    • Chain-of-thought prompting: Asking the model to think step-by-step to solve problems.
    • Providing context: Giving background information so the model better understands requirements.
    • Role definition: Asking the model to act as an expert in a specific field.
