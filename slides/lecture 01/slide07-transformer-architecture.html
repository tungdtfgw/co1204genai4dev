Transformer Architecture

  • Origin & Importance: Introduced in "Attention Is All You Need" (2017), it's now the foundation for most modern LLMs, replacing older architectures like RNNs.
  • Attention Mechanism: The core of the Transformer. It allows the model to weigh the importance of different words in the input, effectively handling long-range dependencies in text.
  • Example: In translation, attention helps the model focus on relevant source words to generate an accurate target sentence.
