Large Language Models (LLMs)

  • Definition of LLMs: Deep learning models trained on massive amounts of text data (billions to trillions of words). Their primary goal is to understand, generate, and manipulate natural language.
  • Role of LLMs in Gen AI: LLMs are the backbone of many text-generating Gen AI applications, including chatbots, writing tools, and especially AI programming assistants. They have the ability to grasp grammar, semantics, style, and even world knowledge from training data.
  • How LLMs Learn from Big Data:
    • Learn to predict the next word in a sequence, or fill in blanks in sentences.
    • This process helps the model build a rich representation of language and knowledge.
