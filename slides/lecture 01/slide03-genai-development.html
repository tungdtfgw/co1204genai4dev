Development Journey of Generative AI

  • Early Beginnings: Ideas about generative AI have existed for a long time, but only truly exploded in recent years thanks to advances in deep learning and computational power.
  • Key Milestones:
    • Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs - 2014): Opened the era of realistic image generation.
    • Transformer (2017): Breakthrough architecture enabling efficient natural language processing, foundation for modern LLMs.
    • Diffusion Models (recent years): Achieved outstanding success in high-quality and diverse image generation.
  • Explosion and Impact: The emergence of models like GPT-3, DALL-E 2, Stable Diffusion has brought Gen AI closer to the public and demonstrated its tremendous potential in many fields, including software development.
