Build Your Own Custom AI Assistant

  • Introduction: Continue is an open-source platform that allows teams to build and share custom AI coding agents. It is designed to be highly customizable and continuously learn from development data.
  • Key Features:
    • Custom AI Coding Agents: Build agents that fit your team's needs and workflows.
    • Centralized Configuration: Manage agent configurations centrally.
    • Secure Credential Management: Manage credentials securely.
    • Model Agnostic: Allows you to choose and connect with any AI model (OpenAI, Anthropic, Ollama, etc.).
    • IDE, Terminal, and CI Integration: Works across different development environments.
  • URL: https://www.continue.dev/
