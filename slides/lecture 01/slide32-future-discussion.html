Future of Gen AI in Software Development

    • Bias in AI Models: How to ensure models don't generate biased code?
  • Opportunities:
    • Accelerated Innovation: Rapidly create prototypes and new products.
    • Democratization of Programming: Enable non-technical people to create software.
    • Solving More Complex Problems: Free humans to focus on bigger challenges.
