Fine-tuning

  • Transfer Learning: Reusing a pre-trained model for a new, related task. This saves time and resources, and improves performance on tasks with limited data.
  • Fine-tuning: Continuing to train a pre-trained model on a smaller, specific dataset. This adapts the model to the nuances of the new data.
  • Example: Fine-tuning an LLM on a company's codebase to generate code that matches its specific style and conventions.
