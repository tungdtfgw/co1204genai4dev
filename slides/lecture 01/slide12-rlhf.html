Reinforcement Learning from Human Feedback (RLHF)

  • Role of Human Feedback: Gen AI models, especially LLMs, can produce inaccurate, harmful, or undesired results. RLHF is a technique used to improve the safety, helpfulness, and appropriateness of these models.
  • RLHF Process:
    • Collect comparison data: Humans evaluate and rank different outputs produced by the model.
    • Train a reward model: This model learns to predict human preferences based on comparison data.
    • Fine-tune the Gen AI model using reinforcement learning: The Gen AI model is fine-tuned to maximize rewards from the reward model, thereby generating results more aligned with human desires.
