The Rise of AI Agents

  • A Mainstream Technology Trend
    AI Agents are considered a key technology trend for 2025. They are seen as a crucial stepping stone towards achieving Artificial General Intelligence (AGI).
  • Beyond Productivity Tools
    Unlike previous AI models like ChatGPT or Gemini that boost productivity, AI Agents fundamentally change how we work. They operate autonomously and make decisions to achieve specific goals set by humans.
