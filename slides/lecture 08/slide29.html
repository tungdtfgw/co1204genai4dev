How the Full System Works

  • A User asks "What was the Magna Carta?"
    1. The input is first sent to the homework_guardrail. 2. The guardrail_agent determines this is a homework question, so the guardrail allows it to pass.
  • The Triage Agent Makes a Decision
    3. The triage_agent now receives the question. 4. It analyzes the question and compares it to the handoff_description of its specialist agents. It decides the "History Tutor" is the best match.
  • Handoff to the Specialist
    5. The triage_agent hands off the question to the history_tutor. 6. The history_tutor processes the question and provides a detailed answer about the Magna Carta.
  • Response Delivery
    7. The history_tutor's response is returned to the user. 8. The system logs the interaction for future reference and improvement.
