A2A Communication: Messages & Artifacts

  • Messages for Continuous Dialogue
    Messages carry the content of an ongoing conversation between agents. They can include instructions, status updates, or intermediate "thoughts" of the agent.
  • Artifacts for Final Results
    Once a task is complete, the result is sent as an "artifact." An artifact is an immutable result, such as a file or structured data.
  • Streaming Support
    A2A supports streaming, allowing agents to send real-time updates and partial results as they work on tasks.
  • Error Handling
    The protocol includes mechanisms for handling errors and exceptions that may occur during agent communication.
  • Status Tracking
    Agents can track the status of tasks and provide updates on their progress through the message system.
