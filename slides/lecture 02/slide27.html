Summary & Key Takeaways

  • Gemini offers multiple models (Pro, Flash, Flash-Lite) with different strengths.
  • Get your API key from AI Studio and use environment variables to authenticate.
  • Install the Gen AI SDK and send your first request with generate_content.
  • Choose between stateless calls and stateful chats depending on your use case.
  • Use system instructions to shape the behaviour of your model.
  • Combine these elements to build your own chat applications.
