Stateless Interactions

  • Use the generate_content method for single-turn interactions. Each call is independent and the model does not retain any conversation history.
  • Stateless calls are ideal for straightforward queries, summaries or transformations where context from previous turns isn’t needed.
  • Because there is no memory, you must pass all necessary information in your prompt.
