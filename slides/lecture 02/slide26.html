Development Tips

  • Keep your API key secret – never hard-code it into source control.
  • Monitor token usage and set appropriate thinking budgets to control cost.
  • Gracefully handle exceptions such as rate limits or network timeouts.
  • Summarise or prune long histories to stay within context limits.
  • Test with different models and parameters to find the right balance of cost and quality.
