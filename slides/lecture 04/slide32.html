OpenAI Prompt Caching - Implementation

  • To leverage caching, divide the prompt into fixed (system prompt, context) and changing (user query) parts.
  • Ensure the fixed part is long enough (>1024 tokens).
  • Always place the fixed part at the beginning of the prompt, as OpenAI only caches the beginning.
  • Keep the fixed part unchanged and identical between requests.
